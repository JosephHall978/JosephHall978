## 🔬 Featured Projects

### 🎙️ [MajorProject](https://github.com/JosephHall978/MajorProject) — Adversarial Attacks on Roblox Voice Moderation
Co-authored research evaluating the robustness of Roblox's WavLM-based Sentinel AI against six adversarial audio attacks (FGSM, PGD, DFT, McAdams). Built an end-to-end evaluation pipeline over a 1,000-sample, 262.9-hour audio dataset; discovered statistically significant **gender bias** in both classifier versions.

`Python` `PyTorch` `torchaudio` `WavLM` `librosa` `scikit-learn`

---

### 🌬️ Offshore Wind Site Evaluation Pipeline — MSc Dissertation
Three-stage automated pipeline for UK offshore wind site selection: constraint mapping, multi-criteria suitability scoring, and cable route optimisation. Integrates UKHO bathymetric data, ERA5 wind reanalysis, CEFAS wave data, and JNCC ornithological surveys into a ranked GeoJSON output.

`Python` `GeoPandas` `GeoJSON` `NumPy` `pandas`
